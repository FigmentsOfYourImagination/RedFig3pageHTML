Figments of Your Imagination

Figments are thoughts that someone believes to be real and unfortunately or fortunately only exist in their imagination

Here are a few examples

  • Any thought that is not based in reality.
  • Imagining that a Demon has been following you your entire life attempting to manipulate you into irrelevant contracts.
  • Picturing a dragon flying through the sky roaring is head down as he breathes out fire

Are these Figments a good or bad thing?
